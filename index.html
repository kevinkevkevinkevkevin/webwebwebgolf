About Us

We are the school golf team. The golf team is very mixed. We have a lot of different age groups, backgrounds, and experience levels. That gives us a lot of potential for improving together and mentoring. This can, however, also create problems when older and younger players stay in their own circle or things like that. Right now there is no structure for us to encourage each other, share tips, or recognise progress, even by just saying “nice shot” from time to time. The goal is simple: play better, feel better, and lift each other up.
